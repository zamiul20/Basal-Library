# Echo variants of bases

1. None
2. Positive : +r,
              -r
2. Negative : +r,
              -r

None:

The number is ordered by increasing or decreasing indexes, from right to left.

Positive:
The number has the indexes systematically placed, where the indexes are ordered based on size, on either side of the 'জ', first batch being on the right hand side.

    +r : The indexes are ordered again on either side, with the smallest indexes being further from the 'জ'.

    -r : The indexes are ordered again on either side, with the smallest indexes being closer to the 'জ'.

Negative:
The number has the indexes systematically placed, where the indexes are ordered based on size, on either side of the 'জ', first batch being on the left hand side.

    +r : The indexes are ordered again on either side, with the largest indexes being further from the 'জ'.

    -r : The indexes are ordered again on either side, with the largest indexes being closer to the 'জ'.

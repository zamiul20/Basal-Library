# Defenitions

Bases are defined with a <`attributes`T or T`attributes`> (there is supposed to be a line over the '<' and '>')

Attributes (one from each row, ordered from rows 1->5), with [a] as the attribute:
1. Type : `The base itself`;
2. Polarity : Regular , Negative `ঋ` , Double Negative `দ` , Positive-Biased Machined Negative `+ম (a)` , Negative-Biased Machined Negative `-ম (a)` ;
3. Format : Regular , Discrete `গ`;
4. Echo : Regular , Positive `+ল a` , Negative `+ল a`;
5. Velocity : 1 , Anything else `স a`

A number could have the indexes's change as an increase to the left or to the right.
